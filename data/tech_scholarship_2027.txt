The Global Tech Scholarship 2027 is a full-tuition program for undergraduate students pursuing degrees in Software Engineering, Data Science, or Robotics. 
Eligibility: Undergraduate students graduating in 2028 or later. Demonstrable financial need or exceptional academic record.
Key areas: Coding excellence, leadership in tech communities, and innovative side projects.
Deadline: January 20, 2027.
Application link: https://global-tech-2027.example.com
