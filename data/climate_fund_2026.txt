The Climate Innovation Fund provides up to $100,000 for startups and research teams developing sustainable energy solutions and carbon capture technologies. 
Eligibility: Registered non-profits, academic research labs, and early-stage startups worldwide.
Key areas: Solar efficiency, Hydrogen fuel cells, and Ocean-based carbon sequestration.
Deadline: October 30, 2026.
